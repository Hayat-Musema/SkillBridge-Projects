About Us

We are a family-owned store in Addis Ababa, dedicated to preserving and sharing Ethiopia's rich artisanal heritage. Every piece in our collection is carefully handcrafted by local artisans using time-honored techniques. From woven baskets to embroidered textiles, each item carries the warmth and story of Ethiopian culture.
